A simple, powerful, open-source Minecraft launcher with instance management, modding features, and more!

Lighter than the vanilla launcher, common alternatives and even Task Manager!

flatpak remote-add quantum-launcher oci+https://mrmayman.github.io/ql-flatpak-remote/

sudo dnf copr enable apicalshark/quantum-launcher-rpm
